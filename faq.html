FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your session. If your question is not here, I would love to help through the booking page.

Ask a Question

Where do you recommend I take my photos?

I love locations with natural light and simple backgrounds, like mountain overlooks, open fields, beaches, and classic city streets. Once you book, I can suggest specific spots based on your style and the look you want.

What should I wear for my session?

Neutral tones, soft earth colors, and simple textures photograph beautifully. Avoid loud logos and super busy patterns. I can help you choose outfits that coordinate well and feel natural in photos.

How long does it take to get my photos back?

Most galleries are delivered within 1-2 weeks depending on season and session type. I always do my best to deliver as quickly as possible while keeping editing quality high.

What if we feel awkward in front of the camera?

That is completely normal and one of the most common concerns people have. I guide posing the whole time with easy prompts so you feel comfortable, relaxed, and confident.

Can I bring outfit changes or props?

Yes. Outfit changes and a few meaningful props can add variety and personality to your gallery. Bring options, and we can choose what works best once we are on location.

What happens if weather is bad?

If weather is too windy, stormy, or dark, we can reschedule to make sure you get the best possible photos. I will stay in communication with you ahead of time and make it easy.
